Tic-Tac-Toe Game

Experience the classic game of Tic Tac Toe in a digital format! In this interactive web application, players can choose to play as either "Player X" or "Player O." The game board is intuitively designed, allowing players to make their moves by clicking on the grid. With a clean and user-friendly interface crafted using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this Tic Tac Toe game provides a fun and engaging way to challenge your friends or enjoy a quick match against the computer.
